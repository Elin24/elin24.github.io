Career Profile

I am a CV researcher based in Hong Kong SAR, China, pursuing the Ph.D. from Video, Image, and Sound Analysis Lab (VISAL), Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, advised by Antoni B. Chan. My Ph.D. research topics encompass crowd analysis, class-agnostic counting, and optimal transport. My research interests lie in the fields of computer vision, deep learning, and machine learning. I am also interested in AI for Science and am looking forward to potential cooperation.

I received Ma.Sc in Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2021, studying in the School of Artificial Intelligence, OPtics and ElectroNics (iOPEN), advised by Qi Wang and Xuelong Li, collaborating with Junyu Gao.
